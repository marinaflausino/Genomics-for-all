Ending the diagnostic odyssey for patients and families across Wales.

Solving the Puzzle

For patients with rare conditions, finding a diagnosis can sometimes take years of tests, misdiagnoses, and hospital visits—a grueling journey often called the "diagnostic odyssey."

Using technologies like Whole Genome Sequencing (WGS), NHS Wales can now look at thousands of genes simultaneously. This allows medical geneticists to pinpoint the exact anomalies responsible for rare developmental or physiological disorders in a matter of weeks, providing rapid answers, peace of mind, and opening doors to specialized support groups and clinical trials.
