Targeted Tumor Therapies

Cancer is fundamentally a disease of the genome. By sequencing the DNA of a specific tumor, doctors can pinpoint the exact genetic mutations driving the cancer's growth in an individual patient.

This allows the All Wales Medical Genomics Service to prescribe targeted therapies. Unlike traditional chemotherapy that affects the whole body and healthy cells, targeted therapies are designed to attack only the cancer cells harboring specific mutations, resulting in higher efficacy and significantly minimized side effects.
